## Space 1: Competitive Intelligence

**Source**: `Competitive Intelligence/Create a competitive positioning matrix comparing.pdf` (7 pages)

### Strategic Positioning

Archevi occupies a **previously underserved quadrant**: family-optimized SaaS tools that don't require technical expertise.

| Quadrant | Players | Archevi Advantage |
|----------|---------|-------------------|
| Generic Cloud Tools | Notion, Google Drive | Lack family-specific controls, treat families as "workspaces" |
| Self-Hosted Privacy | Paperless-ngx | Requires Docker/technical expertise |
| **Family-Optimized Cloud** | **Archevi** | Unique positioning - cloud ease + family design + privacy |

### Competitive Matrix Summary

| Feature | Archevi | Notion | Google Drive | Paperless-ngx |
|---------|---------|--------|--------------|---------------|
| AI-Powered Search | Full (Cohere RAG) | Partial (Business tier) | Basic | Full (OCR local) |
| Family Sharing Controls | Full (Admin/Adult/Teen/Child) | Weak (Guest only) | Full (but no member types) | Weak |
| Document Expiry | Full | None | Partial (links only) | Configurable |
| Multi-Tenant Isolation | Full (by design) | Workspace isolation | Partial (shared drives) | Full (self-hosted) |
| Canadian Privacy (PIPEDA) | Native | US-based (Slack) | US-based | Self-hosted |
| Pricing CAD/month | $9-49 | $0-20+/user | $0-4.99 | Free (self-hosted) |

### 10 Pain Points Archevi Addresses

1. **AI Hallucination Risk** - Notion AI/NotebookLM fabricate details; Archevi's RAG with visibility controls prevents this
2. **Family Access Control** - No competitor has Admin/Adult/Teen/Child member types
3. **US Cloud Privacy Concerns** - PIPEDA issues with Notion/Google; Archevi is Canadian-native
4. **Document Expiry** - Notion has zero; Archevi enables lifecycle management
5. **Non-Technical Onboarding** - Paperless requires Docker; Archevi is SaaS simple
6. **Elder Care Compliance** - No competitor targets legal/regulatory explicitly
7. **Edit Attribution** - No family context in Notion/Google edit history
8. **Person-Centric Organization** - UNIQUE: "Grandpa's documents" filtering
9. **Compliance & Audit Trails** - Weak in competitors; Otter.ai has consent lawsuits
10. **Cross-Border Coordination** - Canadian diaspora needs hard multi-tenant isolation

### Key Takeaway

> **$15B+ TAM gap** in family document management. Archevi captures intersection of cloud convenience + family-centric design + PIPEDA-first privacy.

## Space 8: Technical Landscape & Differentiation

**Source**: `Technical Landscape and Differentiation/Map the technical landscape for AI-powered family.pdf` (21 pages)

### "Goldilocks Zone" Positioning

Archevi sits at the intersection of:
- **Ease-of-use** (unlike Paperless-ngx which requires Docker)
- **Sophisticated AI** (unlike basic cloud storage)
- **Family-first design** (unlike generic productivity tools)

### Multi-Tenant Architecture: STRONGEST Differentiator

| Approach | Used By | Archevi Advantage |
|----------|---------|-------------------|
| Shared database | Notion, most SaaS | Data leakage risk |
| Row-level security | Some enterprise | Complex, error-prone |
| **Dedicated DB per family** | **Archevi** | Hard isolation, PIPEDA-native |

### Technical Stack Validation

| Component | Choice | Validation |
|-----------|--------|------------|
| Vector DB | pgvector | 1.4x lower latency, 21% cost of Pinecone |
| Embeddings | Cohere Embed v3 | 92.4% accuracy (sufficient for family docs) |
| Primary LLM | Groq Llama 3.3 70B | Cost-effective, fast |
| Fallback LLM | Cohere Command-R | Quality backup |
| Reranking | Cohere Rerank v3.5 | Precision on retrieval |
