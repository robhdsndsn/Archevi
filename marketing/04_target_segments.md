## Priority 1: Tech-Savvy Families (Months 1-3)

### Demographics
- **Size**: ~500,000 Canadian households
- **Age**: 35-55
- **Household income**: $80K-150K
- **Education**: College/university
- **Tech comfort**: High
- **Location**: Urban (Toronto, Vancouver, Ottawa)

### Pain Points
- Information scattered across Google Drive, Notion, email
- Paying for multiple subscriptions
- Privacy concerns with Big Tech
- Want control over family data
- Frustrated with "folder hell"

### Psychographics
- Early adopters of new technology
- Value privacy and data ownership
- Cost-conscious but willing to pay for quality
- DIY mentality
- Active on Reddit, HackerNews

### Acquisition Channels
| Channel | Cost | Expected ROI |
|---------|------|--------------|
| Reddit (r/selfhosted, r/homelab, r/privacy) | $0 | High |
| HackerNews | $0 | High |
| ProductHunt | $0 | High |
| Tech podcasts | $0 | Medium |
| GitHub | $0 | Medium |

### Conversion Path
```
Discover via Reddit/HN → Visit website → Start trial → Convert
```

### Expected Metrics
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Acquisition cost | $0-10 (organic) |
| Website → Trial | 10-15% |
| Trial → Paid | 25-35% |
| Lifetime value | $500-800 |
| Churn rate | 5% monthly |

### Messaging Focus
- Privacy and data control
- Cost savings vs alternatives
- Technical architecture (RAG, pgvector)
- BYOK transparency

## Priority 2: Multigenerational Families - Elder Care (Months 4-9)

### Demographics
- **Size**: ~2 million Canadian households
- **Age**: 45-65 (caring for aging parents)
- **Household income**: $70K-120K
- **Family structure**: Adult children + aging parents
- **Location**: Suburban and urban

### Pain Points
- Medical records scattered across clinics, hospitals
- Emergency information not accessible when needed
- Multiple caregivers need coordination
- Fear of missing critical information
- Medication tracking is chaotic
- No single source of truth for care

### Psychographics
- Family-focused, caregiving responsibilities
- Willing to pay for peace of mind
- Less tech-savvy but motivated to learn
- Active on Facebook, community groups
- Trust professional recommendations

### Acquisition Channels
| Channel | Cost | Expected ROI |
|---------|------|--------------|
| Facebook groups (elder care) | $50-100 CAC | High |
| Elder care agency partnerships | Commission | High |
| Estate planning lawyers | Commission | Medium |
| Senior living community partnerships | Commission | Medium |
| Google Ads (elder care keywords) | $5-10 CPC | Medium |

### Conversion Path
```
High pain point → Direct to paid trial → Family tier preferred → Low churn
```

### Expected Metrics
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Acquisition cost | $50-100 |
| Trial → Paid | 40-50% (high pain) |
| Lifetime value | $800-1200 |
| Churn rate | 3% monthly (lower) |
| Preferred tier | Family ($24.99) |

### Messaging Focus
- "Never lose track of medications again"
- Peace of mind and safety
- Caregiver coordination
- Emergency accessibility
- Testimonials from similar families

## Priority 3: Blended Families (Months 6-12)

### Demographics
- **Size**: ~1 million Canadian households
- **Age**: 35-50
- **Family structure**: Remarried with children from previous marriages
- **Household income**: $80K-150K
- **Location**: Suburban

### Pain Points
- Information split across households
- Co-parenting schedule complexity
- Legal documents management (custody agreements)
- Step-family coordination challenges
- Multiple sets of rules and expectations

### Psychographics

- Value clear communication
- Need shared access with boundaries
- Working through complex family dynamics
- Active on parenting forums, Facebook groups

| Family law firm partnerships | Commission | High |
| Family counseling centers | Referral fee | Medium |
| Facebook groups (step-parenting) | Organic | Medium |
| Google Ads (co-parenting keywords) | $5-10 CPC | Medium |

### Conversion Path
```
Pain point discovery → Trial → Extended tier (multiple families) → Low churn
```

### Expected Metrics
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Acquisition cost | $75-125 |
| Trial → Paid | 30-40% |
| Lifetime value | $1000-1500 |
| Preferred tier | Family/Custom |

### Messaging Focus
- "Co-parenting made easier"
- Multiple household coordination
- Legal document access
- Shared yet private spaces
- "Everyone on the same page"

## Geographic Expansion Plan

### Phase 1 (Months 1-6): Ontario & British Columbia
- Largest tech hubs (Toronto, Vancouver)
- Highest concentration of early adopters
- Strong privacy culture
- Target: 80% of initial customers

### Phase 2 (Months 7-12): Quebec
- Requires French translation
- Strong family values culture
- Privacy-conscious market
- Target: 15% of customers

### Phase 3 (Year 2): Rest of Canada
- Alberta, Prairies, Atlantic provinces
- Partner with regional organizations
- Target: 5% of customers

### Phase 4 (Year 2+): International
- United States (similar market, 10x size)
- United Kingdom (privacy-conscious)
- Australia (English-speaking, similar culture)
- European Union (GDPR alignment)
