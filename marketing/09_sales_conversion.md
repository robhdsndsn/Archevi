
## Objection Handling

### Common Objections & Responses

**"It's too technical for my family"**
- Response: "Our managed service handles everything"
- Show: Video of 70-year-old using it successfully
- Offer: Free setup call

**"I'm worried about data security"**
- Response: "Each family gets their own isolated database"
- Explain: BYOK model means we never see your queries
- Offer: Security whitepaper

**"What if I need help?"**
- Response: "Support included with every plan"
- Show: Average response time (<4 hours)
- Offer: Phone support on higher tiers

**"Can I import my existing documents?"**
- Response: "Yes! We support bulk upload"
- Show: Import from Google Drive, Notion, Evernote
- Offer: White-glove migration service ($499)

**"What if the service shuts down?"**
- Response: "Your data is always exportable"
- Explain: No vendor lock-in, standard formats
- Show: Export functionality

**"It's too expensive"**
- Response: Compare to alternatives ($20-40/month)
- Calculate: Annual savings ($384/year vs Notion)
- Offer: Founding member discount

**"Why do I need to bring my own API key?"**
- Response: "That's what makes us truly private"
- Explain: Your queries go directly to Cohere, not through us
- Show: Cost transparency - you see exactly what AI costs

## Retention Strategy

### Onboarding Success
- 30-day check-in email
- "How can we help you get more value?"
- Usage tips based on their documents

### Engagement Triggers
- Weekly digest: "Your family searched X times this week"
- Monthly report: "You saved Y hours this month"
- Quarterly review: "You've stored Z documents"

### Feature Adoption
- In-app tips for unused features
- Email series: "Are you using [feature]?"
- Video tutorials for advanced features

### At-Risk Detection
- No login in 14 days → Email: "Miss anything?"
- Usage declining → Email: "How can we improve?"
- Cancellation intent → Offer discount or pause

### Churn Prevention
- Exit survey (required for cancellation)
- Offer downgrade instead of cancel
- Win-back campaign (30 days after cancel)

### Loyalty Rewards
- Referral program (1 month free per referral)
- Anniversary discounts (on renewal date)
- Beta access to new features
- Exclusive community events

## A/B Testing Priority

### High Priority Tests
1. Headline variations (value prop focus)
2. CTA button text ("Start Free Trial" vs "Get Started Free")
3. Pricing page layout (tiers first vs comparison first)
4. Trial length (7-day vs 14-day)

### Medium Priority Tests
1. Social proof placement (above fold vs below)
2. Video vs static hero image
3. Number of pricing tiers shown
4. Discount display (percentage vs dollar amount)

### Low Priority Tests
1. Button colors
2. Font sizes
3. Testimonial selection
4. FAQ question order

## Sales Playbook (for Family Office tier)

### Qualification Questions
1. What's your family structure? (size, generations)
2. What documents do you need to manage? (volume, types)
3. Who needs access? (family members, staff, advisors)
4. What are you currently using? (pain points)
5. What's your timeline? (urgency)

### Demo Flow (30 minutes)
1. **Introduction** (5 min): Understand their situation
2. **Problem Statement** (5 min): Confirm pain points
3. **Demo** (15 min): Show relevant use cases
4. **Q&A** (5 min): Address concerns

### Follow-Up Sequence
- Day 0: Send demo recording + summary
- Day 2: Check in on questions
- Day 5: Share relevant case study
- Day 7: Proposal with custom pricing
- Day 10: Final follow-up

### Closing Techniques
- Offer pilot program (3 months at reduced rate)
- Include white-glove migration
- Provide dedicated account manager
- Annual contract with discount
