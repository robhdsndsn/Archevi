## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Product not ready | Ship MVP, iterate fast |
| No beta testers | Personal network, paid recruitment |
| Low launch traffic | Multiple channels, not just PH |
| Poor conversion | A/B test, talk to lost trials |
| Negative feedback | Listen, respond, fix publicly |
| Competitor launches | Focus on privacy differentiation |
