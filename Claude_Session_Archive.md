## Archive Organization

This archive preserves the complete historical record organized by:
- **Phases** - Major project phases (Planning, Development, Testing, Deployment)
- **Sessions** - Individual work sessions with full context
- **Outcomes** - Results marked as [COMPLETE], [IN PROGRESS], or [BLOCKED]

### Session Template Format

```markdown
### Session: DATE - Title [STATUS]
**Objective:** What we were trying to achieve
**Context:** Background and relevant information
**Actions Taken:** Step-by-step record
**Issues Encountered:** Problems and challenges
**Resolution:** How issues were solved
**Outcome:** Final result/status
**Key Learnings:** Important insights for future reference
**Files Changed:** List of modified files
```
