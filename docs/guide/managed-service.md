## Data Privacy

Even with managed hosting:
- Your data is **encrypted at rest** and in transit
- We **never read** your family documents
- You can **export all data** at any time
- **PIPEDA compliant** (Canadian privacy law)

## FAQ

### Can I migrate from self-hosted to managed?

Yes! We provide migration tools to transfer your data seamlessly.

### What happens if I cancel?

You'll have 30 days to export your data. After that, it's permanently deleted.

### Where are your servers located?

All data is stored in Canada (Toronto region) on AWS infrastructure.
