# Recipes

Preserve and find family recipes instantly.

## The Problem

- Recipes scattered across cookbooks, cards, and notes
- Handwritten recipes hard to read

- "How do I make grandma's apple pie?"

## Example Recipe Document

**Title:** "Grandma's Apple Pie"

**Category:** Personal

**Content:**
```
Grandma Helen's Famous Apple Pie
Makes: 1 9-inch pie
Time: 1 hour prep, 50 minutes baking

CRUST:
- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup cold butter, cubed
- 6-8 tbsp ice water

FILLING:
- 6 Granny Smith apples, peeled and sliced
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp nutmeg
- 2 tbsp butter

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Mix flour and salt, cut in butter until pea-sized
2. Add ice water gradually until dough forms
3. Divide dough, refrigerate 30 minutes
4. Mix apples with sugar, flour, spices
5. Roll out bottom crust, place in pie dish
6. Add filling, dot with butter
7. Top with second crust, crimp edges, cut vents
8. Bake at 425F for 15 min, then 350F for 35 min

GRANDMA'S TIPS:
- Use a mix of tart and sweet apples
- Freeze butter for flakier crust
- Brush top with milk for golden color
```
