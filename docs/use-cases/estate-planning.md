# Estate Planning

Organize important legal and estate documents for your family.

::: warning Security Note
Estate planning documents are highly sensitive. Ensure proper access controls are in place.
:::

## What to Store

- Will summaries and locations
- Trust information
- Power of attorney details
- Beneficiary information
- Account locations and contacts
- Property deeds and titles

## Sample Questions

- "Where is dad's will stored?"
- "Who is the executor of the estate?"
- "What accounts need to be contacted?"
- "Who has power of attorney?"
